Hype Master (Fall Only)

Every Thursday in the Fall semester there are live recordings in the LAITS studio in MEZ. A live studio audience is recruited each class period. Having up to 20 students in the audience creates a more lively and enthusiastic experience and helps Sam and Paige. Students really enjoy getting to come visit their professors and see a real TV studio in action.

The studio Hype Master is the person who recruits students to join the live audience in the studio each Thursday.

Hype Master Requirements

  • Availability: Must be available to come to the studio in Mezes each Thursday in the Fall by 3:15 PM.
  • Responsibilities:
    • Welcome audience members into the studio with enthusiasm.
    • Hype up the audience and practice clapping for Sam and Paige.
    • Provide instructions to the audience.
    • Stay for the full duration of the lecture.

Students are recruited for the studio audience by selecting a random sample of XX students. INSTRUCTIONS.

Example Email

Here’s a template email you can use to invite students to join the studio audience:

To: yourself
BCC: all students invited

Subject: PSY 301: SAM AND PAIGE NEED YOUR HELP!!

Greetings from the PSY 301 team!

As you may know, Sam and Paige record lectures LIVE every Thursday from right here on the UT campus. We’d like to invite you to join us in our in-person studio audience on [DATE].

You’ll get to sit in our professional TV studio in the Mezes Building (MEZ) and have an in-person learning experience as you watch Sam and Paige skillfully discuss the topics of the day. Students in the studio audience play a very important role - they bring energy, support, and enthusiasm that help Paige and Sam perform their best.

If you’d like to take advantage of this opportunity, please complete the [survey link HERE] to let us know to expect you.

Arrival Details:
- Arrive no later than 3:15 PM on the date above.
- The studio classroom is in Mezes (MEZ), room 2.206.
- Directions:
- Enter the building from the West Main entrance right off the quad.
- Go up a short flight of stairs.
- Follow signs for the Liberal Arts Development Studio to the end of the corridor.
- Wait in line outside the door until a TA comes to get you.

There’s no need to respond to this email - just fill out the form above if you’re interested. We hope to see you there!

Best,
PSY 301 Team

Standby option

Dashboard Runner

Dashboard is the term we use to describe the system we use to launch the various activities in the course. It’s the behind-the-scenes setup of all of the moving parts of a lecture class. The dashboard is set up by the course coordinator before each class and then each aspect of the dashboard is deployed at the appropriate time, so that students can follow each link (such as a chat or benchmark) away from the lecture stream. The person running the dashboard makes each component visible, at the appropriate time, to the students during live lecture. Running the dashboard also involves opening gatekeepers at the right time, so anyone involved in the dashboard setup or running the dashboard will receive extensive training on this process. Below is an image of a dashboard.

Example Dashboard

The Dashboard Runner is the person who is working “live” to deploy all necessary links and activities. (There may be more than one dashboard runner per semester.)

Dashboard Runner Requirements

  • Availability:
    • Fall: Must be available to run the Dashboard on Tuesdays at 12:30-1:55pm or 3:15-4:55pm.
    • Spring: Must be available to run the Dashboard on Tuesday or Thursday from 3:15-4:55pm.
  • Experience:
    • You’ll need to have been a uTA for PSY301 for at least one semester before serving as a Dashboard Runner.

Dashboard Process

Pre-Class Dashboard To-Do List

  1. Set Up Workspace:
    • Open these tabs: video stream (student view), editing dashboard, and Instapoll (if applicable).
  2. Set Up Instapolls:
    • Prepare Instapolls and keep the tab open to launch during class.
  3. Update Dashboard Messaging:
    • Change the dashboard item from “See you next time!” to “Class will begin soon!” by 3:15 PM on lecture days.

During-Class Dashboard To-Do List

  1. Launch Activities:
    • First activity is always Benchmark #. Always update gatekeeper(s) to “start now” when Sam/Paige say “It’s benchmark time.” before making the benchmark available in the dashboard.
      • Note the gatekeeper timer is set to add an addition minute to account for any server delays/timing issues.
    • Change current activity as Sam/Paige transition by clicking the Eye and then save.
  2. Instapolls:
    • For chats: launch the Instapoll in the chat once Sam/Paige says, “Wrap up your chat.”
    • For non-chat polls: send them when instructed by Sam/Paige.

Post-Class Dashboard To-Do List

  1. Wrap Up Dashboard:
    • Change the dashboard message to “See you next time!” after RAS (5 minutes).

Gatekeeper Rules

We use something called Gatekeeping to make sure students don’t have access to certain materials beforehand. Gatekeepers are set by the course coordinator in advance. The Dashboard Runner opens up the assignments.

Opening Gatekeepers

  1. From the Dashboard, copy paste the URL into your browser. This will take you to the particular assignment, for example, a benchmark.
  2. In the upper left, click the drop down “Edit”.

Navigate to Dashboard -> Edit -> Edit Activity Configuration

  1. Click on “Edit Activity Configuration”.

  2. When it is time to launch the assignment, click the “Start Now” check box and click “Save.” (Sometimes this takes a few seconds… We add an extra minute to the time we give the students to account for this.)

Release Assignment with “Start Now”

Gatekept Assignments

  • Benchmarks
  • RASs
  • Coffee Quizzes
  • Coffee Chats
    • We gatekeep these chats because we don’t want students to start chatting early and getting some unfair advantage.

Slack Support

Anytime you run the dashboard, you will not be alone! You will be on Slack in the #class-time channel (This one is locked to only those who need access.) with one of our LAITS super project managers who will be testing everything as you launch and will help with any troubleshooting. ;)
